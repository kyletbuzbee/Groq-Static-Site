Understanding Your Metals

Learn about different metal types, current market values, and how we ensure fair pricing for all materials.

Ferrous Metals

Magnetic metals with iron content

Steel, iron, and other iron-based metals make up the majority of scrap we process. These materials are essential in construction and manufacturing.

Average: 60% of our volume

Non-Ferrous Metals

Valuable metals without iron content

Copper, aluminum, brass, and other non-magnetic metals typically command higher prices per pound due to their industrial value.

Average: 30% of our volume

Precious Metals

High-value specialty commodities

Rare metals like gold, silver, and platinum found in electronics and industrial applications.

Average: 10% of our volume
